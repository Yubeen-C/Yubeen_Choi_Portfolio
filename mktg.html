Cheong-A Skincare Website

The brand name, Cheong-A, comes from the Korean word meaning clear and pure. Cheong-A is an educational skincare website designed to help people of all ages and genders easily access skincare information and discover products that best suit their skin type.
The idea for this project came from recognizing that many people spend a significant amount of time trying to find skincare products that truly work for them. As someone with sensitive skin, I have personally experienced the challenges of choosing suitable products. This inspired me to create a user-friendly platform where visitors can quickly learn about skincare, understand their skin type, and confidently explore products tailored to their individual needs.
